Pottery is a unique form of pottery which is a famous art of the Pink City Jaipur, Rajasthan.The making of a Pottery product is a handmade process and passes through certain stages to get the finished product. It is glazed and low-fired. No clay is used the dough for the pottery is prepared by mixing quartz stone powder, powdered glass, Multani mitti (Fullers Earth), borax, gum and water.
